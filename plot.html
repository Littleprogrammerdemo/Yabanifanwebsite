Short story

The story of Yabani series revolves around a child belonging to a prestigious family. He was kidnapped and live his life on the streets. He tries to regain his old life. The focus of the story is to reveal the reality of relationships. The main question of the story is whether blood relations are stronger than the relationships you build through shared experiences of hardship and pain. The main character of the story, Yaman, has spent his entire life struggling to survive on the streets. There he met three people who were in the same situation. Despite the difficulties they faced in the past, they decided to continue and help each other. They found peace in their unity. The hero of the story collects papers to make a living. However, one day he meets someone and then his life changes and drags him to his lost childhood that he remembers for a long time.

Essence

Yabani is a Turkish television series produced by NTC MEDYA and directed by M. Çağatay Tosun. The series is a drama and was created by Hilal Yıldız. The production company behind the show is NTC MEDYA, whose producers are Fatih Aksoy and Mehmet Yiğit Alp. The series premiered on Tuesday, September 12. The series stars Halit Özgür Sarı, Simay Barlas, Yurdaer Okur, Dolunay Soysert and Bertan Asllani.
